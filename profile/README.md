# Whisper Security

**See the Internet in God Mode**

Whisper is revolutionizing cybersecurity by shifting from reactive defense to predictive intelligence. We empower organizations to anticipate and prevent cyber attacks before they cause damage.

## About Whisper

Whisper provides next-generation, real-time threat intelligence powered by a proprietary knowledge graph that maps over **45 billion interconnected data points** across domains, IPs, ASNs, BGP routing, DNS resolution, and more.

Our platform transcends traditional threat intelligence by:
- **Predicting attacks earlier** - Detect threats before they materialize
- **Cutting false positives** - Advanced graph algorithms deliver exceptional accuracy
- **Understanding threat contexts** - Uncover hidden connections between malicious assets
- **Enabling real-time response** - Live internet infrastructure intelligence

### Why Whisper?

Traditional cybersecurity tools are reactive, siloed, and slow to update, leaving organizations blind to emerging threats. As AI-driven and multi-vector attacks accelerate, Whisper delivers:

- **20x more data points** than traditional threat intelligence platforms
- **6x more source types** including live routing, registration, and resolution data
- **Real-time recency** with continuous infrastructure analysis
- **Predictive capabilities** leveraging advanced graph algorithms and theoretical physics-based scoring models
